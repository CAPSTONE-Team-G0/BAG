Help

What if I entered something wrong?
You can update your information in your profile so BAG can give you more accurate guidance.
My financial aid looks incorrect. What do I do?
Contact your school’s financial aid office for official corrections or questions about your account.
I don’t understand a warning BAG gave me
Review the message and your profile details. If you still need help, your school’s support offices can provide clarification.
Where can I get help right now?
You can contact your school’s financial aid office, veterans office, advising center, or student support services.
