Financial Aid Questions

What is financial aid?
Financial aid is money that helps cover college costs like tuition, fees, books, and other school-related expenses.
What is a disbursement?
A disbursement is when your school releases your financial aid funds and applies them to your student account.
What is a refund?
A refund is money left over after your school pays your tuition and fees using your financial aid.
Does a refund mean I have extra money?
Not always. That money may still be needed for books, transportation, food, and other living expenses.
What happens if I drop below full-time?
Dropping below full-time can reduce or cancel some of your financial aid. Always check before making schedule changes.
