About BAG

What is BAG?
BAG is a student budgeting and guidance app designed to help you manage financial aid across the semester, track your spending, and stay aware of important deadlines and costs.
What can BAG help me do?
BAG helps you organize your aid, track bills, set goals, and understand how your money should be spread across the semester.
Does BAG replace my school’s financial aid office?
No. BAG is a planning tool. Your school still handles official financial aid decisions, disbursements, and account updates.
Does BAG hold or manage my money?
No. BAG is not a bank. It is designed to help you plan and manage how you use your financial aid.
About the team!
BAG was created by Team G0. A team of students who wanted to build a better tool for managing financial aid. We’re passionate about making college finance accessible and transparent for everyone.

We’re a small group of IT students who came together to build something we wish we had as students. Team members J, P, and M focus on programming and development, while team member L handles the database side making sure everything works smoothly behind the scenes. We also teamed up with a graphic design student, E, who helped bring BAG to life visually and shape the user experience. BAG started as our capstone project, but it’s more than just an assignment to us. As we get ready to graduate, we’re excited to keep improving it and growing it into something that can truly help students take control of their finances.
